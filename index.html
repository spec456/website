About us

SPEC is being run under the aegis of Electronics and Communication Department, NIT Hamirpur and the department itself has collaborated in the various activities conducted by SPEC. It's a self governing body run by the students of Electronics and Communications department . SPEC organises events, workshops and educational tours for the students. It is a non profit society run by alloted funds, funds collected from members and sponserships from private organisations. The society holds a prominent position among various societies present in the college. The national level Robotics workshop stands as major event amongst numerous workshops conducted by team SPEC. Other major events include Digital Audio Effects Workshops ,Visit to radio station, Telephone exchanges, Placement Chakra etc. The wall events, Specfest, and other competitions conducted at regular period are major hit among the students. The Team has worked for the benifits of students in past and promise to continue its contribution with full dedication in future......

INITIATIVES

Team SPEC is dedicated to organise workshops and events to make students aware of the various technical knowledge by providing the required resources

Workshop

Team SPEC conducted the ARM Microcontroller workshop, for second ,third and final year students in September'16. This workshop was taken by final year team members under the guidance of OIC SPEC.The well planned workshop helped the undergraduates to test the technicalities with hand-on sessions on kits. It was followed by ARM Design Contest at college level in which 13 teams each consisting of 5 members participated.The exhibhition of projects was arranged in ECE department for students and teachers. Workshop on PROTEUS and PHOTOSHOP software were also conducted in previous academic year for first year students helping them to get familiarized with electronics circuit and it’s implementation. Mass participation of students was witnessed in all workshops.

SPEC fest

Every year Team SPEC organizes 3 day event Called SPEC Fest for the students of NITH to enhance the electronics culture in the society. In previous academic year the events conducted in SPEC Fest were as follows:
ELECTROHUNT
ELECTROMART & ELECTROCOMM
ELECTROBOT
ELECTRO-Q & FELICITATION
CAREER TALK
 On final day of SPEC fest, Team felicitates its Final year team and announces the winners of various events conducted during SPEC FEST .An enthralling, filled with fun and frolic quiz was conducted in auditorium for all years. It was a memorable event and students of NITH look forward to SPEC FEST every year.

guest lectures

Online Guest Lecture arranged by SPEC, where different industrial experts shared their views on ongoing trends in Electronic industry and guidelines for career in ECE.
